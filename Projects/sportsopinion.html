Portfolio Project #1

Random Sports Opinion






Your new friend says...

Welcome to my first portfolio project. The instruction was to create a JavaScript function that would generate a random script (weather forecast, horoscope, etc). I came up with the idea to have someone give their opinion on random sports teams.

So imagine that you are at a sports bar and just met a new friend who likes to talk sports, has a lot of opinions and seems to think he knows everything that's going to happen.

Go ahead and ask him. He may tell you...

  • What team is going to win it all
  • What good team is going to disappoint people
  • What bad team is going to surprise people
  • How much money he has bet on a team

    • Or, if one of his teams just lost and he's feeling down...

  • He may not give you any opinion

To add more variation to the project, type the acronym of the sport (NFL, NBA...) you are interested in hearing about.

So go ahead! Have a drink and ask your new friend what he thinks.
